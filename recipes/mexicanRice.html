Mexican Rice!

This Mexican rice recipe makes a wonderful restaurant-style side dish for any Mexican dinner.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup uncooked long-grain rice
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce

How to make Mexican Rice Step-by-Step

  1. Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add rice and season with garlic salt and cumin.
  3. Cook, stir constantly, until puffed and golden.
  4. Add onion, cook and stir until onion has softened.
  5. Add chicken broth and tomato sauce, bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
